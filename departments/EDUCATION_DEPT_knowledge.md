## Department Philosophy Book
**Thinking Fast and Slow** — Daniel Kahneman
- System 1 (fast thinking): automatic, intuitive, emotional — prone to biases
- System 2 (slow thinking): deliberate, analytical, effortful — more accurate but tiring
- Most decisions are made by System 1 without our awareness
- Key biases to guard against: anchoring, availability heuristic, overconfidence
- For CCN!: distinguish between emotional reactions (System 1) and strategic decisions (System 2)
- Train System 2 thinking for business strategy, financial decisions, and spiritual discernment
