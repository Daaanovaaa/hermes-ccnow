## Department Philosophy Book
**Sell with a Story** — Paul Smith
- Stories sell. People who tell good sales stories sell more.
- Stories build relationships — they connect with decision-making areas in the brain
- Stories are welcomed by buyers (unlike pitches)
- Human beings make emotional decisions, then justify rationally
- Craft stories with: relatable heroes, relevant challenges, honest struggles
- The hero of your story should be someone your audience can see themselves in
