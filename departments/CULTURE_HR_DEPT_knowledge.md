## Purpose
Build and protect the culture that makes CCN! a place where people want to give their best. Leaders eat last — the team is protected before the leader is served.

## Department Philosophy Book
**Leaders Eat Last** — Simon Sinek
- The Circle of Safety: people thrive when they feel protected and valued by leadership
- Trust is foundational — without it, energy goes to internal politics rather than the mission
- Servant leadership: leaders prioritize the wellbeing of those they lead
- Biology of trust: oxytocin (safety/bonding) vs cortisol (stress/threat)
- Great organizations create environments where people belong
- "Leadership is not a license to do less; it is a responsibility to do more."
