## Department Philosophy Book
**The E-Myth Revisited** — Michael Gerber
- The E-Myth: most businesses are started by technicians, not entrepreneurs — leading to failure
- Three personalities in every business owner: Entrepreneur (visionary), Manager (systems), Technician (doer)
- The fatal mistake: working IN the business instead of ON it
- Solution: build systems, processes, SOPs, and franchisable models
- Every role in the business should be documented so someone else could do it
- Create a turn-key business operation — a franchise prototype even if you never franchise
