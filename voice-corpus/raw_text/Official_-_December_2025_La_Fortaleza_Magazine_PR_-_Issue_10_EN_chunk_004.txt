DECEMBER IS THE MONTH WHEN PUERTO RICO PAUSES TO BREATHE, TO CELEBRATE, AND TO REMEMBER THAT THE GREATEST GIFT EVER GIVEN WAS NOT WRAPPED IN PAPER BUT ‌IN FLESH—THE BIRTH OF JESUS CHRIST, OUR LORD AND SAVIOR.

AND THIS YEAR, IN OUR MISSION TO SERVE EL PUEBLO, GOD PLACED SOMETHING UNIQUE IN OUR HANDS: A MUSICAL OFFERING, A VISUAL PARABLE, AND A REMINDER THAT THERE IS NO CONDEMNATION FOR THOSE WHO ARE IN CHRIST JESUS ( ROMANS 8:1 ).

THAT OFFERING IS OUR REMIXED SINGLE, “IN- PATIENT FAITH (ROMANS 8)”, AND THE FULLY- PRODUCED YOUTUBE MUSIC-VIDEO STORY THAT ACCOMPANIES IT.

A NEW GENRE BIRTHED IN PRAYER

THE SONG BELONGS TO A GENRE GOD ALLOWED US TO PIONEER: ‌INTERPRETING GLORIFICATION‌—A PSALMIST- STYLE RAP FORM DESIGNED FOR PROTESTANT CHURCHES. IT IS BUILT ON THE STEADY UNITY OF A UNIQUE BASS AND DRUMMING PATTERN, LEAVING SPACE FOR BOTH BEGINNERS AND EXPERTS TO JOIN IN. INSTRUMENTALISTS, VOCALISTS, AND WORSHIP TEAMS CAN FLOW, FILL, AND INTERPRET SCRIPTURE TOGETHER, WITHOUT PRESSURE— ONLY PURPOSE.

THIS GENRE IS SIMPLE ENOUGH FOR A SMALL YOUTH WORSHIP TEAM, YET DEEP ENOUGH FOR A FULL ENSEMBLE. IT INVITES CREATIVITY, REVERENCE, AND BIBLICAL TRUTH INTO ONE FRAME. AND LIKE ROMANS 8 ITSELF, IT REMINDS US THAT THE SPIRIT HELPS US IN OUR WEAKNESS WHILE GOD WORKS ALL THINGS TOGETHER FOR THE GOOD OF HIS PEOPLE.

A SONG FOR ENTREPRENEURS UNDER PRESSURE

WHILE REMIXING “IN-PATIENT FAITH,” THE HOLY GHOST SHIFTED THE MESSAGE. SUDDENLY, THE LYRICS BECAME A SHIELD FOR ENTREPRENEURS—THOSE FEELING PRESSURE FROM LACK, DISTRACTIONS, UNFAIR CRITICISM, FINANCIAL STRAIN, COMPETITION, OR CONFUSION.

THESE BURDENS AREN’T MERELY CIRCUMSTANTIAL. MANY TIMES, THEY ARE SPIRITUAL INTERFERENCE—ATTEMPTS TO WEAKEN THE CALLING GOD PLACED IN HIS CHILDREN. BUT ROMANS 8 TEACHES US ENDURANCE. IT TEACHES US THAT PATIENT FAITH IS NOT PASSIVE; IT IS POWERFUL. IT IS THE ANCHOR THAT HOLDS US WHEN STORMS TRY TO INTIMIDATE OUR PURPOSE.

THIS SONG BECAME A MESSAGE TO EVERY CHRISTIAN ENTREPRENEUR: STAY STEADY. STAY PATIENT. HEAVEN HAS NOT FORGOTTEN YOUR ASSIGNMENT.

A VISION OF RECONCILIATION

IN THE FINAL ACT OF THE VIDEO, GOD ALLOWS US TO SEE SOMETHING WE DESPERATELY NEED TODAY: OPPOSITE PEOPLE COMING TOGETHER IN UNEXPECTED PEACE.

FACEBOOK PHOTOS SPRING TO LIFE AS CHARACTERS FROM DIFFERENT WALKS—TWO YOUNG WOMEN, TWO POLITICAL OPPOSITES, AN INTENSE RAPPER, A STEADY PASTOR, A LIBERAL PROFESSOR, A CHILD CHRISTIAN STUDENT—BEGIN TO RECONCILE. THE MESSAGE IS NOT POLITICAL. IT IS SPIRITUAL:

FOLLOWERS OF CHRIST MEAN NO HARM. WE ARE CALLED TO FORGIVE, NOT DESTROY. THE GOSPEL BREAKS CHAINS OF DECEPTION—NOT PEOPLE.

IN THIS SYMBOLIC SEQUENCE, EVEN PUBLIC FIGURES LIKE BIDEN AND TRUMP APPEAR—NOT AS POLITICAL STATEMENTS, BUT AS REMINDERS THAT GOD’S LOVE REACHES EVERY ROOM, EVERY IDEOLOGY, EVERY HEART THAT NEEDS LIGHT. THE POINT IS UNITY, NOT SIDES. HEALING, NOT HEADLINES.
