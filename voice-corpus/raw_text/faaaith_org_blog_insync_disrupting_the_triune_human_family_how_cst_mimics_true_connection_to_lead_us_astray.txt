I.N.S.Y.N.C. - Disrupting the Triune Human Family – How CST Mimics True Connection to Lead Us Astray
By
Pastor: Carlos "DaNova" Villanueva Cortes

Pastor: Carlos "DaNova" Villanueva Cortes

This post uncovers how Computerized Summing Technology C.S.T. manipulates emotions and thoughts by creating artificial sync patterns between individuals and their environment. Learn how the I.N.S.Y.N.C. method helps you recognize these manipulations and regain emotional and mental balance, staying aligned with your true self and purpose.
JESUS’ Parable of the Two Builders (Luke 6:46-49)
In JESUS’ parable of the two builders, HE describes two men: one builds his house on rock, the other on sand. When the storms come, the house on the rock stands firm, while the one on sand collapses. This parable can be used to symbolize the difference between a spiritually rooted Christian who builds their life on the WORD OF GOD and one who is easily swayed by the world, including C.S.T. manipulation.
Current Scenario 1: The Conscious Christian
Imagine a man named Daniel who experiences anxiety triggered by a sudden vibration in the atmosphere—like footsteps he thinks are out of sync with his own. In reality, this vibration is created by C.S.T. to manipulate his sense of fear and isolation. Daniel, however, has built his life on the teachings of JESUS. Every morning, he prays and meditates on the WORD OF GOD, which anchors his thoughts in peace. When the vibration occurs, he takes a moment to breathe and refocus on Psalm 23:4, “Even though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death, I will fear no evil, for you are with me.” As he remains calm and grounded in truth, the external manipulation loses its power, and C.S.T.’s hold over his emotions dissipates. The storm passes, and his internal foundation remains firm.
Current Scenario 2: The Preconscious Christian
Many Christians, unaware of quantum mechanics or C.S.T.’s tactics, interpret their inner turmoil as spiritual warfare or divine cleansing. Take Sarah, a woman who constantly feels a sense of dread before important events. She prays, but she attributes her anxiety to GOD “testing” her. Unbeknownst to her, C.S.T. has been sending subtle stimuli through her environment to trigger fearful thoughts and emotional responses. Without realizing it, Sarah’s mind is reacting to external quantum interference, not a test from GOD.
Sarah’s situation mirrors that of the builder who built his house on sand. Her spiritual foundation, while present, is not deeply grounded in understanding the fullness of GOD’s peace. Philippians 4:7 states that the PEACE OF GOD “transcends all understanding and guards your hearts and minds in Christ Jesus.” Had Sarah understood the subtleties of C.S.T. manipulation, she might have sought the wisdom found in GOD’s WORD and overcome the emotional swings.
Current Scenario 3: The Unconscious Person
Dr. Richards, a renowned scientist, has spent his life studying quantum physics and has made several discoveries about particle entanglement. He has a brilliant mind but lacks spiritual insight, focusing solely on the physical world. He perceives quantum phenomena but is unaware of their deeper spiritual implications. While he advances in science, he cannot quite connect the dots between these phenomena and their moral or spiritual consequences.Fallen angels, through C.S.T., allow Dr. Richards to make impressive technological strides while keeping him spiritually blind to the bigger picture. 2 Corinthians 4:4 says, “The god of this world has blinded the minds of the unbelievers, to keep them from seeing the light of the gospel.” Dr. Richards, though highly intelligent, is trapped in a cycle of INCOMPLETE KNOWLEDGE, unable to recognize the higher spiritual reality that governs the quantum phenomena he studies.
Reality could be part of a vast, advanced simulation
Simulation theory posits that our reality might be an artificial construct, similar to a computer simulation. This idea, once speculative, is now being discussed seriously in scientific and philosophical communities. A relevant quote comes from philosopher Nick Bostrom, who argues:"One thing that later generations might do with their super-powerful computers is run detailed simulations of their forebears... If we are interested in creating simulations of humans, why wouldn’t highly advanced civilizations do the same? It is possible that we are living in one of those simulations."Bostrom's simulation hypothesis highlights the increasing acceptance of the idea that our perceived reality could be part of a vast, advanced simulation, especially as technological capabilities continue to grow rapidly.
I.N.S.Y.N.C. Anagram
Let’s create an anagram with the word I.N.S.Y.N.C. that helps students remember how to interpret Current Scenarios where their emotions and will are being manipulated by C.S.T., and how to rely on biblical truth to overcome these manipulations.
Identify: Identify the thought or feeling you’re experiencing. Is it in alignment with the PEACE OF GOD, or does it cause fear, confusion, or dread (2 Timothy 1:7)?
Notice: Notice external stimuli in your environment. Are there sounds, vibrations, or subtle shifts that trigger your emotions (Ephesians 6:12)? These may be manipulated by C.S.T..
Seek: Seek the WISDOM OF GOD. Through prayer and scripture, test whether the thoughts or feelings are rooted in truth (Philippians 4:8). Remember that GOD’s Spirit brings clarity, not confusion.
Yield: Yield to the HOLY GHOST, not to fear or temptation. Romans 8:6 reminds us that the mind governed by GOD is life and peace. Choose to submit your emotions to GOD’s control.
Negate: Negate the power of C.S.T. by renewing your mind in GOD’S WORD (Romans 12:2). When you recognize manipulation, consciously reject the thought or feeling, claiming the authority you have in CHRIST (James 4:7).
Correct: Correct course if necessary. If you’ve acted on a false emotion or thought, turn to GOD for correction and move forward with renewed strength (Psalm 51:10). Acknowledge the misstep and let GOD’s grace guide you back on the right path.
The Harmful Sync Pattern of C.S.T.
One of the most dangerous aspects of C.S.T. is its ability to create what seems like a natural synchronization between human thoughts, emotions, and external stimuli, but this sync pattern is actually designed to cause harm. C.S.T. manipulates the quantum environment around individuals, aligning specific events, sounds, or sensations in a way that appears to be a coincidence or natural occurrence. This creates an illusion that everything happening is “in sync” with the person’s own thoughts or actions, but in reality, it’s an orchestrated interference aimed at disrupting their emotional and mental balance.C.S.T. taps into human memories, emotions, and subconscious fears, adjusting the quantum particles in the atmosphere to manipulate perceptions. For example, a person may hear a strange sound or feel an odd vibration and immediately react with fear, anxiety, or confusion. What feels like an intuitive response is actually triggered by C.S.T., which manipulates the external environment to provoke those specific reactions.
This sync pattern can cause harm in subtle and overt ways:
Emotional Disruption: By creating artificial emotional triggers, C.S.T. destabilizes inner peace and well-being, often leading to feelings of dread, anxiety, or paranoia.
Physical Consequences: These quantum sync patterns can also cause accidents or impulsive behavior—such as running into the street, falling down stairs, or making harmful decisions—because the brain perceives a threat that isn’t real but has been artificially introduced.
Relational Discord: C.S.T. can use sync patterns to create emotional disconnects in relationships, where family members or close friends feel out of tune with one another. What might seem like natural disagreements or misunderstandings is actually manipulation of their emotional and mental states.
The Bible warns that Satan is “the ruler of the power of the air” (Ephesians 2:2), which indicates that much of this manipulation happens in the unseen realm of quantum particles and waves that flow through the air around us. While it may be invisible to the naked eye, the effects are very real, and understanding how C.S.T. operates can help us remain vigilant and protected.
Resisting Harmful Sync Patterns
Christians must remain alert to the tactics of C.S.T. and its attempts to create sync patterns that manipulate our thoughts and emotions. The key to resisting this manipulation is found in Romans 12:2, which urges us to “be transformed by the renewing of your mind.” By anchoring our minds in GOD’s truth and being conscious of both spiritual and scientific realities, we can break free from harmful patterns and walk in the peace and freedom that CHRIST offers.
Conclusion
In the quantum realm, where C.S.T. seeks to infiltrate human consciousness, it is the WORD OF GOD and the power of the HOLY GHOST that protect and guide believers. By training students to use the I.N.S.Y.N.C. anagram and grounding their faith in scripture, they can discern manipulation, resist temptation, and remain steadfast in their spiritual walk.This balance of science and faith not only equips individuals to understand their external environment but also empowers them to overcome through the STRENGTH OF CHRIST, who alone fulfills and anchors the soul.
