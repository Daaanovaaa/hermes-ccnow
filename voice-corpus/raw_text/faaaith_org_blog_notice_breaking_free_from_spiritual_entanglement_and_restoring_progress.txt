N.O.T.I.C.E. - Breaking Free from Spiritual Entanglement and Restoring Progress
By
Pastor: Carlos "DaNova" Villanueva Cortes
6 Oct 2024
Pastor: Carlos "DaNova" Villanueva Cortes
6 Oct 2024
In our daily lives, we often encounter challenges that can derail our progress—both in the natural and spiritual realms. For believers, it's crucial to stay spiritually vigilant, using the wisdom GOD has given us to identify when we are under attack. This is where the concept of N.O.T.I.C.E. comes in—a tool that helps us recognize and respond to spiritual entanglements.
Current Scenario 1: Triune Human Beings in Action
A close-knit group of friends is working on a community project, creating a space for local entrepreneurs and artists. Suddenly, one of their members starts feeling overwhelmed and defeated. Instead of brushing it off, the group recognizes that this friend has been entangled by an adversarial force. Without hesitation, they N.O.T.I.C.E. the attack:
Navigate: They remain spiritually alert and stay on course.
Observe: They recognize the signs of a spiritual attack—fear, doubt, and confusion.
Testify: One of them speaks up, sharing that they sense a disruption in their friend’s peace.
Intercede: They immediately pray together, rebuking the negative influence.
Connect: They lean on the HOLY GHOST and offer emotional support to their friend.
Evolve: Together, they grow in spiritual maturity, overcoming the attack and returning to their work, unshaken.
By relying on GOD and each other, they not only break free from the quantum based spiritual entanglement but also resume their project with renewed focus and energy. In this way, they fulfill the biblical charge of James 4:7: "Submit yourselves therefore to God. Resist the devil, and he will flee from you."
Current Scenario 2: A Group Unaware of a Quantum Based Spiritual Battle
Meanwhile, another group of friends finds themselves in a similar situation. They're working on an important project, but one member starts getting irritable, and soon, the entire group is drawn into an argument. Tempers flare, and instead of working together, they blame each other for the setbacks.
What they don’t realize is that their frustration is not just a natural emotion but part of a quantum based spiritual entanglement. Without the awareness or tools to resist the adversary, they lose valuable time, effort, and even their unity. Instead of addressing the root cause, they internalize the anger and shame, unable to see that an external adversary has manipulated their emotions.
This group ends up spending more energy on arguments and negative emotions than on their actual work, losing both progress and peace of mind. Sadly, their project stalls, and they fail to N.O.T.I.C.E. the spiritual forces at play.
Biblical Insight: Power Over Fear
2 Timothy 1:7 (KJV) reminds us that "God hath not given us the spirit of fear; but of power, and of love, and of a sound mind." In both current scenarios, fear and confusion crept in, but only the first group recognized that these emotions weren’t from GOD. They had the courage to resist and reclaim their peace.
Scientific Connection: Quantum Entanglement
As in quantum entanglement, where two particles are interconnected, spiritual entanglements can affect an entire community. When one person is spiritually under attack, it can impact the whole group. By noticing and addressing the issue, the group breaks free, much like particles responding to each other from afar. This interconnectedness is a reflection of our spiritual and emotional ties.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Quantum_entanglement
Psychological Reference: Cognitive Reframing
From a psychological perspective, reframing the situation is key. The first group reframed their problem from "we’re stuck" to "we’re under attack but can overcome this together." Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) teaches us that reframing our thoughts leads to better emotional responses and decision-making, much like the renewal of the mind in Romans 12:2.
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cognitive_reframing
In conclusion, through faith, spiritual awareness, and even scientific and psychological principles, we can recognize and break free from quantum based particle entanglements that threaten our progress. When we N.O.T.I.C.E., we are equipped to move forward with power, love, and a sound mind.
