La Fortaleza PR USA was operating on a 2015 college- era MacBook. The mission was advancing, but the workflow was crawling. Deadlines were tightening. Systems were aging. Stewardship required capacity. A Facebook Marketplace listing appeared: a Dell 3420 for $200. A simple outreach message was sent. Kaleb McCary responded. He lowered the price to $150. The meeting was set immediately in Aguada because the March issue was in active completion. What began as a transaction became a testimony of alignment. During the meeting, ministry was discussed. Vision was shared. The governance of the HOLY GHOST over La Fortaleza PR USA was explained plainly and without theatrics. Kaleb paused. Then he said he felt from GOD ALMIGHTY to give the Dell 3420 freely. No negotiation. No pressure. No emotional appeal. He asked only for prayer. Payment was attempted through ATH Móvil. It was refused. Repeatedly. He stated he too is a believer, and that obedience mattered more than money. So we prayed. The manifestation was not dramatic — it was ordered. Provision replaced strain. Capacity replaced limitation. Stewardship was upgraded in real time.

On the drive home, something unexpected occurred. The phone rang. It was Kaleb again. He said he also had a Dell 5440. And he wanted to donate that as well. Return to Aguada. Another meeting. Another transfer. Another act of conscious obedience. For nearly ten years, La Fortaleza PR USA operated without modern equipment. In a single evening, through one man’s responsiveness to the HOLY GHOST, a decade-long limitation was removed. This was not charity. This was not coincidence. This was stewardship aligning with manifestation. Real Reliable Conscious Faith is not loud. It is not performative. It does not require spotlight. It is obedience in motion. Kaleb McCary demonstrated that faith is reliable when it is conscious — aware of GOD’S prompting, and responsive without hesitation. The laptops were not the miracle. The governance was. And from that night forward, La Fortaleza PR USA entered a modern workflow capacity that directly impacted Issue 13 and future expansion initiatives. Kaleb said he simply wanted to bless the world. In March 2026, he blessed it by strengthening the infrastructure of a mission already in motion.
