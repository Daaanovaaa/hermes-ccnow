LOS 78′ — FEBRUARY 2026 Choose Your Circle: A FREE ENERGY‌™️

Field Test in Municipio #14 In the MIGHTY NAME of KING JESUS CHRIST‌👑‌.

Welcome to Municipio #14. We won’t tell you which one yet—because that’s part of the adventure. It’s 8:17 a.m. in Puerto Rico, USA‌🇵🇷🇺🇸‌. The café has strong coffee, weak Wi-Fi, and surprisingly strong faith.

You’re about to witness a real-world simulation of what happens when a Circle of Friends‌™️‌ decides to stop competing… and starts cooperating.

Choose Your Role‌ ‌👇

Pick one. Don’t overthink it.‌

A) The Pastor with a Podcast‌ B) The Baker with a Brand‌ C) The Videographer with Too Many Ideas‌ D) The Mayor’s Intern Who “Accidentally” Stayed for Prayer

(Whatever you pick—you’re already in the Circle.)

The Cast (Fictional… but not unbelievable)‌

Pastor Joel Rivera — hosts Café y Escritura, a 12-minute devotional podcast recorded in his car‌

María Luz Torres — owner of Pan del Reino, an artisan bakery with kingdom branding and zero ad budget‌

Alex “Lens” Santiago — Christian videographer, allergic to spreadsheets, loves drones‌

FREE ENERGY‌™️‌ Circle of Friends‌™️‌ — 11 believers across Puerto Rico, each with a phone, a platform, and a testimony

No one here is famous. Everyone here is faithful.

The Moment of Alignment (Spirit Signal™ Detected)

No business plan was opened.

No algorithm was consulted.

Someone simply said:

“What if this month… we lift María?”

Silence. Then nods.

This is how FREE ENERGY‌™️‌ actually activates—not by hype, but by agreement.

The Rotation Begins ‌🔄

Center Stage: Pan del Reino

For the next 14 days, the Circle chooses one focus:

👉‌ Pan del Reino — María’s bakery 👉‌ Online pre-orders + affiliate links 👉‌ A limited-run product: “El Pan Diario”

No rush. No pressure. Just alignment.

Decentralized Action (a.k.a. Everyone Does Their Thing)‌

Pastor Joel mentions the bread once at the end of his podcast‌ Alex cuts three short vertical videos (one accidentally funny)‌ Someone’s aunt shares the link on WhatsApp‌ Another member posts: “Supporting local + Kingdom economics today ‌🍞 ✝️‌”

No scripts. No micromanaging.

Physics lesson: Many small forces → one shared direction → multiplied impact

That’s FREE ENERGY‌™️‌.

The Results (Nobody Brags… but Everyone Notices)

Orders ship to 9 municipalities María hires her cousin part-time Circle members earn modest commissions (yes, real money) FREE ENERGY™️ infrastructure is sustained Most importantly: confidence increases

Someone says quietly: “Wait… so when it’s my turn… they’ll do this for me?” Yes.

That’s literally the system.

Why Los 78′ Cares

Los 78′ was never about fantasy. It was about influence through story. This fictional-but-functional scenario shows how:

Municipal economies can modernize Faith can lead innovation Quantum ideas (many nodes, one field) can break religious ice CHRIST remains the center, not the branding

Puerto Rico doesn’t need louder voices. It needs aligned ones.

Choose Your Ending ✨

If you chose A (Pastor): Your congregation now understands economics without fear If you chose B (Baker): You’re no longer building alone If you chose C (Creative): Your gift finally has direction If you chose D (Intern): You just witnessed a better policy model

Or maybe… You’re choosing to start a Circle.
