Empowering the Future: How a Progressive Christian Multimedia Company is Redefining Life with 'Funny, Money, Honey'
By
Pastor: Carlos "DaNova" Villanueva Cortes

Pastor: Carlos "DaNova" Villanueva Cortes

Discover how a progressive Christian multimedia company is reshaping lives with a unique blend of humor, financial empowerment, and personal well-being.
Transforming Lives Through Progressive Christian Multimedia
At Conscious Culture NOW!, we believe in the transformative power of progressive Christian multimedia content. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for individuals and communities by providing a fresh perspective that integrates fun, financial prosperity, and personal well-being.
Our content is designed to be accessible and engaging, offering innovative videos, compelling scripts, and strategic partnerships that uplift and inspire. Rooted in Christian values, our approach is inclusive, aiming to make a positive impact on anyone seeking a more fulfilling life.
The Power of Humor: Embracing the 'Funny' Factor
Laughter truly is the best medicine, and at Conscious Culture NOW!, we prioritize humor as a key component of our content. The 'Funny' pillar is all about infusing joy and adventure into everyday life through comedy shows, creative storytelling, and playful skits.
Our humorous approach not only provides a sense of relief from life's challenges but also aligns with Christian values. By encouraging viewers to find joy in the mundane and embrace adventure, we aim to uplift spirits and foster a sense of excitement and positivity.
Proverbs 17:22 “A merry heart doeth good like a medicine: but a broken spirit drieth the bones.”
Financial Empowerment: Unpacking the 'Money' Factor
Financial independence is crucial for a fulfilling life, and our 'Money' pillar focuses on empowering individuals to achieve financial success. Through Christian tech-based business strategies, we provide aggressive yet accessible methods for financial growth.
Collaborating with entrepreneurs, we offer insights into modern business approaches. Our multimedia content equips viewers with the tools, knowledge, and motivation to take charge of their finances, start businesses, and create sustainable income—while staying true to their Christian faith.
Proverbs 10:22 “The blessing of the LORD, it maketh rich, and he addeth no sorrow with it.”
Holistic Well-being: The Essence of the 'Honey' Factor
Personal well-being is at the heart of our 'Honey' pillar. We address mental, physical, and sexual health openly and honestly, recognizing that true confidence comes from feeling comfortable in all areas of life.
Our progressive Christian approach covers topics like mental health, relationships, and sexual health in a respectful and empowering way. By promoting openness and self-acceptance, we encourage viewers to embrace their uniqueness and navigate life with strength and confidence.
Genesis 2:24 “Therefore shall a man leave his father and his mother, and shall cleave unto his wife: and they shall be one flesh.”
Building a Better Future: The Impact of Conscious Culture NOW!
Conscious Culture NOW! is committed to building a better future by reshaping lives through our unique blend of humor, financial empowerment, and holistic well-being. Our content is more than just entertainment; it's a tool for personal and community transformation.
By addressing essential aspects of life with a progressive Christian lens, we aim to inspire, educate, and uplift. Our impact is seen in the positive changes within individuals and communities who engage with our multimedia offerings, leading to a more joyful, prosperous, and confident future.

Pastor: Carlos "DaNova" Villanueva Cortes
Carlos 'DaNova' Villanueva Cortés Jr. was born and raised in Chicago, Illinois, but his roots are in Mayagüez, Puerto Rico. He is a multifaceted professional, blending his expertise in ministry, media production, and community leadership. While living in Jacksonville, Florida, Carlos hosted the TV show Conscious Culture NOW! for 12 episodes, bringing progressive Christian values to the forefront. He is also a positive rap artist who actively promotes community hip-hop values among fellow artists, using music as a tool for connection and empowerment.
