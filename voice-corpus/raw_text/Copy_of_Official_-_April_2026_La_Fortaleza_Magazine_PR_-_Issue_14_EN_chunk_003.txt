THE GARDENER THE FAITH JOURNEY OF PASTOR JAMES

DURDEN OF BIBLE DAY OUTREACH

MINISTRIES

EARLY ROOTS IN STILLMORE I WAS BORN AND RAISED IN A SMALL RURAL COMMUNITY IN STILLMORE, GEORGIA, ON A FAMILY FARM. WE RAISED LIVESTOCK AND WORKED THE LAND, GROWING COTTON, CORN, TOBACCO, AND SOYBEANS. I LEARNED THE VALUE OF HARD WORK FROM A VERY EARLY AGE. WE HAD NO RUNNING WATER—WE DREW IT FROM A WELL—AND NO INDOOR TOILET, BUT WE ENDURED. AT THE AGE OF THIRTEEN, I JOINED THE FIRST BAPTIST CHURCH OF STILLMORE, WHERE I SERVED FAITHFULLY FOR OVER SEVEN YEARS AS A CHOIR MEMBER, USHER, SUNDAY SCHOOL SUPERINTENDENT, AND DEACON. EVEN WHILE HOLDING THESE POSITIONS, I DID NOT TRULY KNOW JESUS IN MY HEART. DURING THOSE YEARS IN THE CHURCH, I WAS STILL LIVING IN SIN. I STRUGGLED DEEPLY WITH DRUG ADDICTION—PILLS, MARIJUANA, COCAINE, AND CRACK—AS WELL AS ALCOHOLISM AND PROMISCUITY. THIS LIFESTYLE CONTINUED FOR MANY YEARS. IN THE 1980S, I SPENT TIME IN JAIL DUE TO MULTIPLE DUI CHARGES.

THE TURNING POINT:

A CRY FROM PRISON

WHILE INCARCERATED IN 1989, I HAD A POWERFUL SPIRITUAL ENCOUNTER WITH THE SPIRIT OF THE LORD. IN THAT MOMENT, THE ANGEL MICHAEL APPEARED TO ME AND BEGAN SHOWING ME THE WAYS OF THE LORD. EVEN THOUGH I HAD BEEN IN CHURCH FOR YEARS, THE ANGEL TAUGHT ME HOW TO RECOGNIZE THE ENEMY, SATAN. I ALSO LEARNED HOW TO DEFEAT THE ENEMY DURING THIS SEASON. AFTER BEING INCARCERATED FOR 85 DAYS, I WAS RELEASED, BUT I HEARD THE LORD CLEARLY WARN ME: “IF YOU DO THE SAME THING AFTER YOU GET OUT, YOU WILL BE BACK LOCKED UP AGAIN.” SURE ENOUGH, AFTER THREE MONTHS OF FREEDOM, I RETURNED TO PRISON ON THE SAME DUI HABITUAL VIOLATOR CHARGE. THIS CYCLE OF GOING BACK AND FORTH TO JAIL AND PRISON LEFT ME EXHAUSTED. IN 1991, AFTER ANOTHER RELEASE, I TRIED ATTENDING VOCATIONAL SCHOOL AND WAS DOING WELL—UNTIL ONE DRINK OF ALCOHOL SENT ME BACK BEHIND BARS. IN MY DESPERATION, I PRAYED EARNESTLY: “LORD, WHATEVER IT TAKES FOR ME TO TRULY KNOW YOU, I AM WILLING TO DO IT—EVEN IF IT MEANS GOING BACK TO PRISON.” THE FOLLOWING YEAR, I WAS SENTENCED TO FIVE YEARS WITHOUT PAROLE. DURING THAT TIME, I SERVED ONE YEAR WITH NO POSSIBILITY OF EARLY RELEASE: THREE MONTHS IN COUNTY JAIL AND NINE MONTHS IN PRISON. I NOW CALL THAT SEASON MY “NEW BIRTH.” AS 2 CORINTHIANS 5:17 DECLARES: “THEREFORE, IF ANY MAN BE IN CHRIST, HE IS A NEW CREATURE: OLD THINGS HAVE PASSED AWAY; BEHOLD, ALL THINGS BECOME NEW.”

SALVATION AND THE CALL TO

EVANGELISM

ON JULY 7, 1992, WHILE IN MAXIMUM SECURITY, I ACCEPTED JESUS CHRIST AS MY PERSONAL SAVIOR. MY LIFE CHANGED FOREVER. I HUNGERED TO KNOW MORE ABOUT THE LORD AND THE HEAVENLY REALM. THE HOLY SPIRIT BEGAN TEACHING ME THE WAYS OF JESUS, AND I LEARNED TO FOLLOW CHRIST WITH ALL MY MIND, HEART, AND SOUL. MY SPIRITUAL JOURNEY CONTINUED AFTER MY RELEASE FROM PRISON ON FEBRUARY 1, 1993. THE SPIRIT OF THE LORD CALLED ME TO DO THE WORK OF AN EVANGELIST AND MAKE FULL PROOF OF MY MINISTRY. ON DECEMBER 23, 1993, DURING A SHUT-IN AND FAST, I RECEIVED THE GIFT OF THE HOLY GHOST. I HAVE NOT BEEN THE SAME SINCE—I WAS TRULY BORN AGAIN. FROM 1994 TO 1997, I TRAVELED UP AND DOWN THE EASTERN COAST OF THE UNITED STATES, FROM MIAMI TO BOSTON, SPREADING THE GOSPEL OF JESUS CHRIST. IN APRIL 1994, I CAME TO JACKSONVILLE, FLORIDA, WITH A PROPHET WHO TOLD ME I WOULD DO MINISTRY WORK ON THE EASTSIDE, WHERE HEAVY CRIME EXISTED. AS A SMALL-TOWN COUNTRY BOY, I INITIALLY RESISTED THE IDEA.

April 1997

July 7, 1997.

~ THE ARRIVAL
& DELIVERANCE

OBEDIENCE AND THE WILDERNESS

IN APRIL 1997, THE LORD SPOKE TO ME CLEARLY: TURN IN MY TWO WEEKS’ NOTICE AT MY JOB AND MOVE TO JACKSONVILLE ON APRIL 14. THOUGH I DIDN’T WANT TO GO, I OBEYED AND RESIGNED. ON JULY 7, 1997—EXACTLY FIVE YEARS AFTER MY SALVATION—I MADE THE DECISION TO HEAD TO JACKSONVILLE WITH NO MONEY AND NO CHANGE OF CLOTHES. I CAME WITH ONLY WHAT WAS ON MY BACK. IT WAS MY “JONAH EXPERIENCE.” WHEN I ARRIVED, I ASKED THE LORD WHERE HE WANTED ME TO GO. HE LED ME TO THE VERY PLACE THE PROPHET HAD SPOKEN OF. THERE, I WAS DIRECTED TO A CRACK HOUSE, AND THAT IS WHERE I BEGAN THE MINISTRY. IN THAT MOMENT, I WAS INSTANTLY DELIVERED FROM CRACK COCAINE. I LEARNED TO LEAN AND DEPEND COMPLETELY ON JESUS. HIS WORD BECAME LIFE TO ME, AND I KNEW HE WOULD NEVER LEAVE NOR FORSAKE ME.

THE HARVEST TODAY

DURING THIS MINISTRY, WE HAVE BEEN HELPING THE LESS FORTUNATE WITH FOOD AND CLOTHING, AS WELL AS SHARING THE UNADULTERATED WORD OF GOD. WE ALSO GROW FRESH VEGETABLES IN OUR GARDEN. WE SEEK TO MEET THE NEEDS OF THE WHOLE PERSON—BOTH THEIR PHYSICAL AND SPIRITUAL NEEDS. WE ARE HERE TO SERVE IN WHATEVER CAPACITY THE HOLY SPIRIT LEADS. WHEN YOU TRULY TRUST THE WORD OF GOD, THERE IS NOTHING HE WON’T DO FOR YOU ACCORDING TO HIS WILL.
