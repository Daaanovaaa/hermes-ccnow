About Conscious Culture NOW!
Founded by Carlos "DaNova" Villanueva Cortes, Conscious Culture NOW! is a progressive Christian entertainment company rooted in faith and innovation. We create inspiring multimedia content, live events, and merchandise that reflect Protestant Christian values while embracing modern technology and creativity. With a global community focus, we aim to engage audiences through accessible digital platforms, unique experiences, and a $1 subscription model.
Carlos, a licensed pastor, artist, and entrepreneur, leads the team with a diverse background in music, media production, and business. Together, we’re building a movement that merges entertainment, faith, and culture, aspiring to one day launch an international Christian theme park, offering an immersive space for families and believers to grow spiritually through cutting-edge technology.
