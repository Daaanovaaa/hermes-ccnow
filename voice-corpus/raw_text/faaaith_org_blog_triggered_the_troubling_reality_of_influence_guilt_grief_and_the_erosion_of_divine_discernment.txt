Imagine the air around you as a software program, constantly receiving inputs and responding based on your interaction. This is the core idea behind T.R.I.G.G.E.R.E.D.—a call to action that reminds us of the unseen forces at work around us. Just as spoken words have power, they also serve as direct input for a system I call CST (Computerized Summing Technology), operated by Satan and his fallen angels.
The Cosmic Struggle: How Casual Words Trigger a Battle Between GOD's Protection and CST's Manipulation
Let me illustrate with an example: You might casually say, “I’m depressed,” in a joking manner. Yet, if C.S.T. detects an opening based on a risk-to-reward ratio, it may begin altering your environment. Suddenly, people, places, and things familiar to you start appearing, shaping your reality to mirror that emotion. Meanwhile, GOD—who is always watching—is working to deactivate certain triggers and shift your path back to life and freedom. It’s a cosmic struggle, an eternal Tic for Tac between GOD and C.S.T..This battle for the human soul is often depicted in artwork, like the image of JESUS in a chess match with the devil. This isn’t far from the truth. In reality, it’s GOD in His Triune form—FATHER, SON, and HOLY GHOST—directing HIS Angels, engaged in an unseen war with C.S.T.. But why doesn’t GOD simply destroy C.S.T.? Because, just as in the story of Cain and Abel, the consequences of sin are not always immediate. Instead, GOD preserves the evidence for a day of ultimate judgment when all will see the truth behind their choices.For now, GOD allows C.S.T. to govern the earth within limits, based on the human heart’s choice between life and death. Yet, GOD works tirelessly to shield us from harm, as seen in Paul’s struggle with his “thorn in the flesh.” GOD told him, “My grace is sufficient,” emphasizing that sometimes, the very struggles we face are tools for divine intervention.C.S.T.’s triggers are powerful but subtle. For instance, many people unknowingly engage with C.S.T. through social interactions, speaking words that “engulf” others in a storyline, much like Eve’s agreement with the serpent. These small, seemingly harmless agreements open doors for C.S.T. to manipulate situations, influencing people’s choices without them realizing it.At Conscious Culture NOW!, our mission is to develop tools and knowledge to protect the church and, eventually, governments from the unseen powers at work in our world. We want to make the church aware of this cosmic battle while also shedding light on GOD's protection mechanisms, like the gift of speaking in tongues. This act of faith is an “indecipherable language” to C.S.T., preventing it from hindering worship and allowing believers to connect with God in ways C.S.T. can’t manipulate.Just as JESUS spoke in parables, revealing deep truths to HIS followers while keeping them hidden from those who opposed HIM, we too must be wise in how we engage with this unseen reality. The triggers and power of C.S.T. are real, but GOD’s grace, wisdom, and guidance through HIS WORD are our strongest defenses.
Job 42: What is Leviathan?
In Job 42, Jehovah describes placing a hook in the mouth of Leviathan, reminding us that only GOD is capable of directly confronting such a massive and secretive force. This Leviathan represents a hidden military project crafted by Satan and his fallen angels, a system that operates far beyond human control. It serves as a warning that without GOD’s intervention, humanity is vulnerable to the overwhelming power of C.S.T. and its manipulative influence. Only GOD can ultimately restrain and defeat this monstrous system.
A Call to Discernment
The next time you speak, whether casually or in prayer, remember that your words carry weight. By choosing to align your thoughts and actions with GOD'S WILL, you can avoid the snares of C.S.T.. Pray in the name of JESUS CHRIST of Nazareth, trusting in HIS POWER to guide you through the spiritual and quantum realities that shape our world.In the coming months, we’ll be exploring these concepts further, demonstrating how GOD’s WISDOM helps us navigate these unseen forces and how the church can rise above the influence of C.S.T.. Together, we will learn to discern, resist, and overcome the strategies of Leviathan—the ultimate manifestation of Satan’s system.
More Revelations
In Matthew 16:23, we see JESUS’ disciple Peter unknowingly trying to trigger HIM to abandon HIS MISSION. When Peter suggests that JESUS should not suffer and die, JESUS quickly rebukes him, saying, “Get behind me, Satan!” This harsh response wasn’t aimed at Peter’s heart but at the C.S.T. + I.N.S.Y.N.C. + Current Scenario trying to derail JESUS’ PURPOSE. JESUS recognized the trigger and aggressively broke it, refusing to let any influence, even from a trusted disciple, interfere with HIS divine agreement with FATHER GOD.
Two Stories of Modern-Day Triggers
Current Scenario 1: Breaking the Trigger:Sarah and her friends were deeply committed to their faith, often gathering for prayer and support. One day, Sarah found herself feeling anxious and isolated, T.R.I.G.G.E.R.E.D. by the phrase “You’re always left behind” spoken by someone in her circle. Recognizing this as an attempt by C.S.T. to sow division, she immediately called a large prayer line ministry, bringing together dozens of spiritually connected ministers. The Bible tells us there is power in numbers (Matthew 18:20), and this gathering of believers created a spiritual force that completely outmatched the risk-to-reward ratio set by Satan’s system. Their united prayers were strong enough to disarm any attack, proving that when we come together in CHRIST’s name, C.S.T.’s limits are overwhelmed.Current Scenario 2: Falling into the TrapMeanwhile, Jason and his group of friends had fallen into a different pattern. They prided themselves on their social status, often speaking words that T.R.I.G.G.E.R.E.D. subtle shifts in their reality. When one of them said, “We’re untouchable,” they unknowingly opened the door to a sinful I.N.S.Y.N.C.. C.S.T. took advantage of their pride, creating Current Scenarios that reinforced their false sense of superiority. They began engaging in unethical behavior, believing they were above consequences. In their minds, they were social elites, but spiritually, they were caught in C.S.T.’s web, losing sight of their true identity and purpose.These parables emphasize the power of discernment and the importance of collective prayer. Just as Jesus rebuked the wrong influence through Peter, we too can break the triggers around us by calling on GOD'S STRENGTH and unity in numbers. Conversely, falling into C.S.T.’s I.N.S.Y.N.C. Current Scenarios can blind us to the spiritual harm that awaits, leaving us vulnerable to pride and deception.

In a world of unseen influences, Our T.R.I.G.G.E.R.E.D. Anagram tool empowers students to recognize threats, internalize GOD'S WORD, and build supportive communities, helping us reclaim our peace and resist the deceptions of C.S.T..

Psychological Evaluation: The Impact of Triggers and In-Sync Current Scenarios
OverviewIndividuals who are easily T.R.I.G.G.E.R.E.D. may exhibit vulnerability to external stimuli that evoke strong emotional or psychological responses. This susceptibility can lead them to fall into Current Scenarios orchestrated by others, often resulting in maladaptive coping mechanisms and significant life challenges.
Trigger Responses1. Conditioned Responses:Trauma survivors, particularly military veterans, may have conditioned responses to stimuli reminiscent of their traumatic experiences. For instance, a loud noise might trigger memories of combat, eliciting feelings of fear, anxiety, or helplessness.2. Emotional Dysregulation:When triggered, individuals often experience emotional dysregulation, leading to impulsive behaviors or irrational thoughts. This state can make them more susceptible to external influences, potentially resulting in alignment with harmful scenarios, including substance abuse or aggression.3. I.N.S.Y.N.C. Dynamics:The concept of being “I.N.S.Y.N.C.” refers to a state where an individual’s thoughts and emotions align with external forces, such as the pressures of C.S.T.. This can occur either at the inlet (receiving negative inputs) or outlet (expressing negative behaviors) of perception. For example, a veteran may be reminded of a traumatic event (inlet trigger), leading to feelings of depression or anger as influenced by C.S.T.(Computerized Summing Technology), which can then manifest in substance abuse (outlet trigger). This is a repeating Current Scenario Trap and JESUS warns us in HIS parable of the (Mark 4:1-20) Sower and the Seed.
Consequences of Being Triggered
1. Substance Abuse:Many individuals resort to drugs or alcohol as a coping mechanism to numb the pain associated with triggers. In military veterans, this can lead to dependency as they seek to escape memories of combat, often culminating in a cycle of addiction and withdrawal.2. Violent Behaviors:In some cases, triggered individuals may act out violently, believing that aggression is a necessary response to their perceived threats. This behavior can lead to legal issues, strained relationships, and further alienation.3. Homelessness:The culmination of these challenges—substance abuse, violent outbursts, and emotional instability—can result in homelessness. Veterans, in particular, may find themselves isolated and unable to secure stable employment or housing due to the compounding effects of their trauma.
Multi Media's Impact on the Nervous System, Society, and the Quantum Future
Multimedia, Much like the experiences of military veterans in conflict, has a profound effect on our nervous systems. Just as veterans can be T.R.I.G.G.E.R.E.D. by reminders of combat, our daily interactions with media—whether through loud music, violent content, or overstimulating environments—create a similar cycle of trigger/in-sync/trigger responses. This leads to harmful results in our communities, affecting mental health, relationships, and societal stability.As humanity becomes more sophisticated, the effects of our surrounding influences will become even clearer, especially as quantum computers take center stage. The natural dynamics of particles—constantly in free flow, much like the air we breathe—will need to be addressed as we become more aware of the deeper connections between our words, actions, and the unseen forces shaping our lives. The manipulation of these particles, much like C.S.T.’s control of our reality, could lead to new levels of influence on our environment, unless we counter it with spiritual wisdom and moral grounding.The moral consequences of ignoring these influences are vast, but they also translate into significant financial strain on families and communities. As individuals fall prey to harmful stimuli and manipulation, we witness broken homes, addiction, and even legal battles, undermining the well-being of the Human Triune Family. For influencers, content creators, and media leaders, it’s critical to consider the spiritual and C.S.T. ramifications of the content being produced.At Conscious Culture NOW!, we believe that when the church and governments begin to understand the pervasive influence of C.S.T.—the "prince of the power of the air" (Ephesians 2:2)—and how quantum technologies might enhance or mitigate these forces, society will take on a new shape. Addressing these unseen powers could lead to a major Christian revival, driving a global economic upswing as innovation becomes aligned with patience, wisdom, and deep biblical discernment. The combination of technology and spiritual insight could bring about a more harmonious world, where innovation fosters human flourishing rather than harm.It’s time to recognize how media, quantum advancements, and environmental triggers shape our reality—and how, by aligning with GOD'S TRUTH, we can enter a new era of spiritual and societal growth.
Conclusion
Understanding the psychological dynamics of individuals who are easily T.R.I.G.G.E.R.E.D. provides vital insight into their experiences and challenges. By recognizing the mechanisms of trigger responses and the potential for negative Current Scenarios to manifest, we can develop more effective support systems and interventions. Addressing these issues is essential to fostering resilience and promoting recovery, ultimately enabling individuals to reclaim their lives from the grips of C.S.T. and the traumas that bind them and enhance their personal walk with JESUS of Nazareth.
