A.N.G.E.R.-The Anti-Anger Campaign: Understanding Anger in a Quantum-Controlled World

Pastor: Carlos "DaNova" Villanueva Cortes

Anger is a powerful emotion that can lead individuals down a destructive path, affecting physical health, emotional stability, and spiritual well-being. Left unchecked, anger has the potential to spiral into situations where people lose control, experience physical ailments such as strokes, or even fall into sin by blaspheming against GOD. As we navigate the complexities of modern life, it is crucial to understand that anger is often a tool used by negative forces, such as fallen angels and their technological weapon, C.S.T. (Computerized Summing Technology), to undermine our lives.
This campaign will shed light on how these dark forces manipulate our emotions, causing chaos in our relationships and clouding our judgment. As Christians, we must remain vigilant, discerning the source of our frustrations and guarding our hearts from these external influences. And while understanding the science behind these manipulations is important, we must also look to the spiritual solutions JEHOVAH FATHER GOD has already provided through JESUS CHRIST and the HOLY GHOST.
Anger: A Weapon of Manipulation
Throughout scripture, we are warned about the dangers of anger. James 1:20 (KJV) tells us, “For the wrath of man worketh not the righteousness of God.” Anger can quickly lead to actions that separate us from GOD, from hurting others to even damaging ourselves. But beyond the emotional and spiritual effects, anger can also serve as a portal for dark forces to manipulate our reality.
Through C.S.T., Satan and his fallen angels have developed advanced quantum technology capable of influencing the smallest particles of matter, including our emotions. This technology operates both internally and externally. Internally, it uses quantum mechanics to track and analyze human thoughts and behaviors. Externally, it manipulates our surroundings—people, places, and things—to create scenarios that provoke anger and discord.
ref. Job 1:12 - 1:19
By understanding how C.S.T. works, we can learn to discern these manipulations and take control before falling into destructive behaviors. Here’s a closer look at how two different couples handled a similar scenario involving C.S.T.
Current Scenario 1: The Discerned Couple
Jacob and Sarah had been married for ten years. Through their faith in CHRIST and study of quantum science, they were familiar with the manipulative tactics of C.S.T. They understood how easily Satan could interfere with their emotions and reality, creating tension in their relationship.
One evening, Jacob noticed the scent of roses from the backyard. Drawn to the aroma, he walked outside and saw their young neighbor tending her flowers. Meanwhile, inside the house, Sarah felt an unexpected impulse to check on Jacob. Through C.S.T.’s manipulation, she saw him in the garden near their attractive neighbor, and jealousy stirred within her.
However, Sarah was prepared. She had learned to N.O.T.I.C.E. these triggers, remembering 1 Peter 5:8 (KJV): “Be sober, be vigilant; because your adversary the devil, as a roaring lion, walketh about, seeking whom he may devour.” She prayed for clarity, asking the HOLY GHOST to reveal the truth. At the same time, Jacob sensed a shift in the atmosphere and invited the HOLY GHOST into his heart, also praying for wisdom.
In that moment of prayer and discernment, they both realized that C.S.T. was at work. Instead of letting anger or jealousy take over, they sat together and calmly discussed their feelings, recognizing that their emotions were being manipulated. Because they had trained themselves to be aware of Satan’s schemes, they avoided a fight and instead strengthened their bond.
Current Scenario 2: The Undiscerned Couple
Mike and Lucy, a young couple, lacked the spiritual awareness and knowledge of C.S.T. that Jacob and Sarah possessed. One day, while Lucy was preparing dinner, she suddenly felt an urge to check on Mike, who was outside.
C.S.T. had orchestrated the situation perfectly. As Lucy looked out the window, she saw Mike standing near their young neighbor, who happened to be outside at the same time. A wave of jealousy overtook Lucy, fueled by memories of infidelity stories from a TV show she had watched the previous day.
Without pausing to reflect, Lucy rushed outside and accused Mike of being unfaithful. Shocked and hurt, Mike responded defensively. Neither of them realized they were being manipulated by external forces, and their argument escalated quickly, with both saying hurtful things they would later regret.
Unlike Jacob and Sarah, Mike and Lucy hadn’t learned how to N.O.T.I.C.E. the subtle manipulations of C.S.T. Their lack of discernment led them into a destructive fight, damaging their relationship and opening the door to further conflict.
The Solution: The HOLY GHOST and Spiritual Discernment
As followers of JESUS CHRIST, we are not powerless against these attacks. In fact, JEHOVAH FATHER GOD, in His infinite wisdom, has already provided the solution through HIS SON, JESUS. JESUS taught His disciples that after He ascended to heaven, the FATHER would send the HOLY GHOST to guide them into all truth (John 14:26, KJV).
The HOLY GHOST, who now dwells within believers, helps us discern between GOD’s reality and the manipulations of C.S.T. Even though quantum physics and technology were not fully understood at the time of CHRIST’s earthly ministry, today we can cooperate with GOD’S HOLY GHOST more effectively by recognizing patterns of manipulation and shielding ourselves from these dark influences.
When we feel anger rising within us, it is crucial to pause and pray, inviting the HOLY GHOST to intervene. By doing so, we can prevent C.S.T. from manipulating our emotions and driving us into sinful actions. Instead, we can respond with grace, peace, and love—the very qualities that reflect the HEART OF GOD.
The A.N.G.E.R. Anagram
As part of the Anti-Anger Campaign, let’s remember that A.N.G.E.R. can be broken down into:
Acknowledge: Recognize the root of your anger. Understand that it may be influenced by external forces like C.S.T. or negative emotions designed to provoke conflict. Be aware that anger often isn't from GOD and needs to be discerned.
Navigate: Instead of reacting impulsively, navigate through the situation by asking the HOLY GHOST for guidance. Pause and reflect on the situation, allowing spiritual clarity to lead your actions.
Guard: Guard your heart and mind with scripture. Verses like James 1:19 remind us to be "slow to anger," which will protect you from destructive outbursts that open doors to manipulation.
Exhale: Physically and emotionally release the tension. Take deep breaths, pray for peace, and let the HOLY GHOST fill you with calming reassurance that overrides the pressure of the moment.
Reset: Reset your perspective through prayer and communion with the HOLY GHOST. Shift your focus from the negative emotions to the love, patience, and peace of GOD, allowing you to return to the situation with a clear, unshaken mind.
This is a reminder that anger is never from GOD and cannot lead to productive, healthy lives. As soon as we feel ourselves growing angry, we must N.O.T.I.C.E. the situation for what it is—an external manipulation—and seek GOD’s peace instead.
The Physical Consequences of Anger: A Medical Doctor’s Perspective
From a medical perspective, anger can have a severe impact on the body. Dr. Charles Spielberger, a clinical psychologist specializing in anger, explains that when a person becomes angry, the body goes through a series of physiological changes. The adrenal glands release stress hormones like adrenaline and cortisol, and the brain’s amygdala, which regulates emotional responses, is activated.
Medical research shows that these physiological changes can result in the following harmful effects:
Heart and Blood Pressure Issues: Frequent anger episodes cause blood pressure to spike and heart rate to increase. Over time, this can lead to cardiovascular problems like hypertension, heart disease, and even strokes.
Weakened Immune System: Continuous exposure to high levels of stress hormones like cortisol weakens the immune system, making the body more susceptible to infections and slower healing.
Digestive Problems: Anger affects the digestive system, contributing to issues like acid reflux, ulcers, and irritable bowel syndrome (IBS).
Increased Risk of Stroke: Anger has been linked to a higher risk of stroke. Studies suggest that within two hours of an angry outburst, a person’s risk of a stroke can increase due to blood clotting and constriction of blood vessels.
Understanding the physical consequences of anger underlines the importance of managing it.
Just as Proverbs 14:29 (KJV) advises, “He that is slow to wrath is of great understanding: but he that is hasty of spirit exalteth folly,” slow, thoughtful reactions protect the body and soul.
Anger from a Psychiatrist’s Perspective
From the viewpoint of a psychiatrist, anger is not just a fleeting emotion but often a manifestation of deeper psychological issues. According to Dr. George Simon, a clinical psychologist, anger can be categorized into two main types:
Reactive Anger: This is an immediate response to perceived threats or frustrations. It is impulsive and often uncontrolled.
Chronic Anger: This refers to long-standing feelings of resentment or bitterness, which can lead to ongoing mental health problems like anxiety and depression.
Psychiatrists often use specific techniques and terminologies to help patients manage and check anger:
Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT): A widely used therapeutic approach, CBT helps individuals recognize patterns of thinking that lead to anger and develop healthier, more constructive responses.
Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR): This involves teaching patients to focus on the present moment and observe their emotional responses without judgment, helping them to calm their reactions.
De-escalation Techniques: Psychiatrists often teach de-escalation strategies to prevent anger from building up, including deep-breathing exercises, progressive muscle relaxation, and cognitive reframing (changing the way you perceive a situation).
Dr. Richard Tafrate, a clinical psychologist specializing in anger management, emphasizes that understanding the “anger triggers” and the body’s physical warning signs are essential. He encourages patients to notice the early signs of anger, such as clenching fists, increased heart rate, or feelings of tension, and to apply coping strategies immediately.
James 1:20 (KJV) reminds us that “the wrath of man worketh not the righteousness of God,” underscoring the importance of managing anger before it leads to destructive behavior, both mentally and spiritually.
JEHOVAH GOD’s Solution to Anger and the Battle with C.S.T.
In response to the temptations and negative influences brought forth by C.S.T., JEHOVAH GOD has already provided the ultimate solution in the birth, life, death, and resurrection of His only begotten SON, KING JESUS CHRIST of Nazareth. JESUS taught us that He would return to heaven, where GOD the FATHER would send HIS HOLY GHOST to indwell believers, guiding them away from destructive forces like C.S.T. Through this divine connection, we are empowered to recognize when our emotional states are being manipulated and can choose paths of righteousness and peace.
In today’s world, where technology and quantum manipulation are realities, cooperating with GOD’s HOLY GHOST allows us to navigate these challenges effectively. Anger Is Not From GOD
Anger is a destructive emotion that serves no purpose in a healthy, productive life. Whenever we feel angry, it is essential to remember that this emotion is not from GOD but from forces that seek to undermine our health, well-being, and spiritual growth.
By learning to recognize anger’s physical and psychological signs and applying spiritual wisdom and modern scientific understanding, we can prevent anger from controlling our lives. JESUS of Nazareth came to redeem humanity from confusion, sin, and destructive emotions, and through HIS teachings and the guidance of the HOLY GHOST, we can live lives free from the bonds of anger.

In our world, where quantum technologies like C.S.T. are influencing our emotions and circumstances, it is more important than ever to stay rooted in our faith. By understanding how these dark forces operate and by relying on the HOLY GHOST, we can discern the manipulations of C.S.T. and avoid falling into anger and destruction.
