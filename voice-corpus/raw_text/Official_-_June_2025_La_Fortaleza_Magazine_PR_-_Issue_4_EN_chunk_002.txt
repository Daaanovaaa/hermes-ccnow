THE ANSWER LIES NOT IN THE VISIBLE, BUT IN THE INVISIBLE QUANTUM ARRANGEMENT OF OUR LIVES. WE ARE CONSUMING A SET OF PARAMETERS NOT OF OUR OWN DESIGN. THE SPIRITUAL, CULTURAL, ECONOMIC, AND PHYSICAL VARIABLES WE LIVE BY ARE NOT NATIVE TO OUR ESSENCE OR OUR LAND—THEY ARE IMPOSED FROM AFAR. PUERTO RICO HAS UNKNOWINGLY BECOME A TESTING GROUND OF FOREIGN EXPECTATIONS, STYLES, AND STRUCTURES THAT SIMPLY DO NOT FIT OUR NATURAL ENVIRONMENT OR OUR ‌GOD‌-GIVEN IDENTITY.

WE ARE NOT NEW YORK. WE ARE NOT PORTLAND.

THIS ISLAND IS NOT BUILT FOR SKYSCRAPERS, GLASS BUILDINGS, FAST FASHION, IMPORTED PLASTIC, OR ROBOTIC HEALTHCARE. WE ARE A GIANT GREENHOUSE, A LIVING, BREATHING EDEN FULL OF MEDICINAL PLANTS, NATURAL RHYTHMS, AND ABUNDANT BEAUTY. YET WE’VE PAVED OVER GOD’S GARDEN WITH CONCRETE, AND WE DRESS LIKE WE’RE IN CITIES THOUSANDS OF MILES NORTH —IGNORING OUR HUMIDITY, SUN, AND TERRAIN.

WE SHOULD BE DEVELOPING SHOES, CLOTHES, HATS, GLASSES, JEWELRY, VEHICLES, AND HOMES DESIGNED SPECIFICALLY FOR LIFE ON THIS ISLAND. OUR EDUCATION SYSTEM SHOULD TRAIN STUDENTS IN ISLAND-SPECIFIC INNOVATION: TEXTILES THAT BREATHE IN OUR CLIMATE, METALS THAT DON’T CORRODE IN SALT AIR, TRANSPORTATION THAT WORKS IN MOUNTAINOUS AND COASTAL AREAS ALIKE, AND ‌HOLISHTIC‌ TOURISM THAT HEALS BOTH VISITORS AND LOCALS.

IT’S TIME TO BUILD OUR OWN PARAMETERS

WE MUST REAWAKEN TO A HOLY GHOST-LED, QUANTUM- CONSCIOUS CULTURE. A CULTURE THAT DOES NOT WAIT FOR FOREIGN IMPORTS TO DEFINE WHAT IS VALUABLE, BUT RATHER, USES INTELLIGENT LOCAL TECHNOLOGY TO IDENTIFY WHAT’S FREE AND ABUNDANT—SOIL, SUN, HERBS, WATER, STORIES, MUSIC, FAMILY, FAITH—AND BUILDS WITH THEM. WE MUST STOP MIMICKING OTHER PLACES AND EMBRACE THE TRUTH: WE ARE ALL THE PLACE WHERE WE LIVE.

WE MUST ENGINEER OUR ECONOMY AND SOCIETY BASED ON TRIPLET QUANTUM SYSTEMS—ARRANGEMENTS WHERE THREE ALIGNED REALITIES (SPIRIT, SOUL, AND BODY-LAND) REINFORCE EACH OTHER, RATHER THAN CONTRADICT ONE ANOTHER. CURRENTLY, OUR SYSTEMS ARE FRACTURED: OUR DIET, OUR HEALTHCARE, OUR EDUCATION, OUR SPIRITUAL LIFE—ALL OUT OF SYNC. THIS IS WHY SO MANY ARE SICK. THE SOUL, BODY, AND ISLAND ARE VIBRATING IN DIFFERENT FREQUENCIES. WE MUST UNIFY THEM.

A HEALTH SYSTEM THAT HEALS OR HURTS?

LOOK NO FURTHER THAN OUR MEDICAL INDUSTRY— FRAGMENTED, COMPETITIVE, AND DEHUMANIZING. WE HAVE A TINY POPULATION—3.2 TO 3.5 MILLION PEOPLE—AND YET DOZENS OF COMPETING HEALTH PLANS. DOCTORS WASTE PRECIOUS TIME ARGUING OVER CO-PAYS AND PAPERWORK INSTEAD OF HEALING. WORSE, THESE FRACTURED SYSTEMS OFTEN EXCLUDE THE PATIENT FROM THE DECISION-MAKING PROCESS, CREATING A SINGLET QUANTUM SYSTEM—AN ISOLATED LOOP WHERE THE HUMAN BEING, THE MOST IMPORTANT VARIABLE, IS REMOVED FROM THE HEALING EQUATION.

THIS IS DANGEROUS. IT LEADS TO MISCOMMUNICATION, UNNECESSARY MEDICATIONS, EXPENSIVE TREATMENTS THAT DON’T WORK, AND WORST OF ALL: A COLLAPSE OF TRUST. DOCTORS BEGIN TREATING CHARTS, NOT PEOPLE. THEY CONSULT EACH OTHER WITHOUT EVER TALKING TO THE PATIENT. WHAT WE NEED IS ONE UNIFIED, COMPREHENSIVE MEDICAL PLAN ACROSS THE ISLAND. ONE HEALTH LANGUAGE. ONE MEDICAL STANDARD. ONE GOAL: HEALING.

NO MIDDLE CLASS WITHOUT A MIDDLE MISSION

A STRONG SOCIETY CANNOT EXIST WITHOUT A MIDDLE CLASS, AND A MIDDLE CLASS CAN’T EXIST UNLESS ESSENTIAL WORKERS ARE PAID LIKE ESSENTIAL PEOPLE. POLICE OFFICERS SHOULD BE MAKING $83,000 A YEAR MINIMUM. TEACHERS, NURSES, ENGINEERS—THE SAME. THIS CANNOT HAPPEN UNLESS OUR UNIVERSITIES STOP COPYING HARVARD OR UCLA AND INSTEAD GO DEEP INTO PRODUCING ISLAND-BASED PROFESSIONALS WHO SUPPLY THE ISLAND’S OWN VITAL NEEDS.

WE MUST TRAIN OUR YOUTH TO BE PROCURERS OF LOCAL RESOURCES—FROM TEXTILE MANUFACTURING TO ETHICAL TOURISM, FROM ECO-FRIENDLY TRANSPORTATION TO CULTURALLY ROOTED EDUCATION. IMPORTING IS NOT EVIL, BUT IT’S ONLY WISE ONCE WE HAVE SELF-SUSTAINING SYSTEMS IN PLACE AND A PEOPLE WHO KNOW EXACTLY WHO THEY ARE.

GOD’S DESIGN, OUR ASSIGNMENT

WE ARE NOT HERE BY ACCIDENT. WE ARE NOT BROKEN. WE ARE NOT VICTIMS. WE ARE GOD’S APPOINTED STEWARDS OF THIS LAND. JESUS CHRIST HAS ALREADY GIVEN US THE WISDOM, THE INTELLIGENCE, AND THE IDENTITY TO THRIVE HERE—NOT SURVIVE. BUT THAT REQUIRES A CONSCIOUS RE-ENGAGEMENT WITH THE NATURAL PARAMETERS OF THIS ISLAND—OUR VALUES, OUR CLIMATE, OUR SPIRIT, OUR LIMITATIONS, AND OUR POWER.

WE ARE NOT A SICK PEOPLE. WE ARE A PEOPLE MISALIGNED WITH THEIR OWN SOURCE CODE.

LET’S REALIGN. LET’S REBUILD. LET’S RESET OUR TRIPLET QUANTUM ARRANGEMENT.

FOR THE LOVE OF THIS LAND, THIS PEOPLE, AND OUR GOD- BLESSED FUTURE.

PUERTO RICO—LA ISLA DEL ‌CORDERO‌, LA PERLA DEL CARIBE—SE HA CONVERTIDO EN UNA PARADOJA: UN PARAÍSO DONDE NUESTRA GENTE ESTÁ PERECIENDO EN SILENCIO. ¿CÓMO ES POSIBLE QUE EN ESTA TIERRA EXUBERANTE, BAÑADA POR EL SOL Y ACARICIADA POR EL MAR, ESTEMOS SUFRIENDO BAJO UNA OLA ANTINATURAL DE ENFERMEDADES Y DISFUNCIONES? DIABETES, CÁNCER, DERRAMES CEREBRALES, ENFERMEDADES MENTALES, MIEDO, ANSIEDAD, PÉRDIDA DE MEMORIA, DISFUNCIÓN SEXUAL, IRA CRÓNICA—Y LO MÁS EXTRAÑO, UN PATRÓN DE ACCIDENTES DONDE TANTO JÓVENES COMO MAYORES SE CAEN, TROPIEZAN O RESBALAN DE FORMA TAN REPETIDA QUE YA NO PARECE CASUALIDAD.

NO SOMOS NUEVA YORK. NO SOMOS PORTLAND.

ESTA ISLA NO FUE HECHA PARA RASCACIELOS, EDIFICIOS DE CRISTAL, MODA RÁPIDA, PLÁSTICOS IMPORTADOS O SALUD ROBÓTICA. SOMOS UN INVERNADERO GIGANTE, UN EDÉN VIVIENTE LLENO DE PLANTAS MEDICINALES, RITMOS NATURALES Y BELLEZA ABUNDANTE. Y, SIN EMBARGO, HEMOS PAVIMENTADO EL JARDÍN DE DIOS CON CONCRETO, Y NOS VESTIMOS COMO SI ESTUVIÉRAMOS A MILES DE KILÓMETROS AL NORTE—IGNORANDO NUESTRA HUMEDAD, SOL Y GEOGRAFÍA.

DEBERÍAMOS ESTAR DESARROLLANDO ZAPATOS, ROPA, SOMBREROS, GAFAS, JOYERÍA, VEHÍCULOS Y VIVIENDAS ESPECÍFICAMENTE PARA VIVIR AQUÍ. NUESTRO SISTEMA EDUCATIVO DEBERÍA PREPARAR A LOS ESTUDIANTES CON INNOVACIÓN ISLEÑA: TEXTILES QUE RESPIREN CON NUESTRO CLIMA, METALES QUE NO SE OXIDEN CON LA BRISA SALINA, TRANSPORTE ÚTIL TANTO EN MONTAÑAS COMO EN LA COSTA, Y TURISMO HOLÍSTICO QUE SANE A VISITANTES Y A RESIDENTES POR IGUAL.

ES HORA DE CONSTRUIR NUESTROS PROPIOS PARÁMETROS

DEBEMOS DESPERTAR A UNA CULTURA CONSCIENTE CUÁNTICA GUIADA POR EL ESPÍRITU SANTO. UNA CULTURA QUE NO ESPERE POR IMPORTACIONES PARA DEFINIR LO QUE TIENE VALOR, SINO QUE USE TECNOLOGÍA LOCAL INTELIGENTE PARA IDENTIFICAR LO QUE YA ES LIBRE Y ABUNDANTE—TIERRA, SOL, HIERBAS, AGUA, HISTORIAS, MÚSICA, FAMILIA, FE—Y CONSTRUIR CON ELLO. DEBEMOS DEJAR DE IMITAR A OTROS LUGARES Y ACEPTAR LA VERDAD: SOMOS EL LUGAR DONDE VIVIMOS.

DEBEMOS DISEÑAR NUESTRA ECONOMÍA Y SOCIEDAD BASÁNDONOS EN SISTEMAS CUÁNTICOS TRIPLES—ARREGLOS DONDE TRES REALIDADES ALINEADAS (ESPÍRITU, ALMA Y CUERPO-TIERRA) SE REFUERCEN MUTUAMENTE, EN LUGAR DE CONTRADECIRSE. ACTUALMENTE, NUESTROS SISTEMAS ESTÁN FRACTURADOS: NUESTRA DIETA, SALUD, EDUCACIÓN Y VIDA ESPIRITUAL ESTÁN FUERA DE SINCRONÍA. POR ESO HAY TANTA ENFERMEDAD. EL ALMA, EL CUERPO Y LA ISLA ESTÁN VIBRANDO EN FRECUENCIAS DISTINTAS. DEBEMOS UNIFICARLOS.

SIN CLASE MEDIA, NO HAY MISIÓN MEDIA

UNA SOCIEDAD FUERTE NO PUEDE EXISTIR SIN UNA CLASE MEDIA, Y ÉSTA NO PUEDE EXISTIR SI LOS TRABAJADORES ESENCIALES NO RECIBEN SALARIO ESENCIAL. LOS POLICÍAS DEBERÍAN GANAR MÍNIMO $83,000 AL AÑO. MAESTROS, ENFERMEROS, INGENIEROS—IGUAL. ESTO NO SERÁ POSIBLE MIENTRAS NUESTRAS UNIVERSIDADES SIGAN IMITANDO A HARVARD O UCLA EN VEZ DE FORMAR PROFESIONALES ARRAIGADOS EN LAS NECESIDADES VITALES DE NUESTRA ISLA.

DEBEMOS ENTRENAR A NUESTRA JUVENTUD COMO PROVEEDORES DE RECURSOS LOCALES—DESDE FABRICACIÓN TEXTIL HASTA TURISMO ÉTICO, DESDE TRANSPORTE ECOLÓGICO HASTA EDUCACIÓN CON RAÍCES CULTURALES. IMPORTAR NO ES PECADO, PERO SÓLO ES SABIO CUANDO YA TENEMOS SISTEMAS AUTOSUSTENTABLES Y UN PUEBLO QUE SABE EXACTAMENTE QUIÉN ES.
