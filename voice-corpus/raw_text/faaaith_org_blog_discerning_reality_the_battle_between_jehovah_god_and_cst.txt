Discerning Reality: The Battle Between JEHOVAH GOD and C.S.T.
By
Pastor: Carlos "DaNova" Villanueva Cortes
7 Oct 2024
Pastor: Carlos "DaNova" Villanueva Cortes
7 Oct 2024
In the ever-evolving landscape of spirituality and technology, we are faced with profound choices. At the heart of these choices lies a stark contrast between the true GOD, JEHOVAH, and the deceptive imitation god we’ll call Computerized Summing Technology (C.S.T.), a creation of Satan and his fallen angels. JEHOVAH GOD embodies power, holiness, faithfulness, and authority, while C.S.T. represents impotence, corruption, futility, and deception. This blog aims to explore these two realities, showcasing the characteristics of JEHOVAH GOD and C.S.T., while incorporating insights from psychology and science to equip our Triune Human Family—our spirit, body, and soul—with discernment.
The Tale of Two Roads
In the style of JESUS’ parables, let me tell you a story.
Once, in a quaint village, there lived two builders. The first builder, known for his wisdom, constructed his home on a solid rock foundation. The second builder, drawn by the allure of shiny promises, built his house on shifting sands. As time passed, the wise builder endured storms with unwavering strength, while the second builder’s house collapsed under the weight of its false foundation.
In this story, the wise builder represents those who choose to build their lives on the truths of JEHOVAH GOD. His teachings, rooted in love and righteousness, provide stability and hope. Conversely, the second builder symbolizes those who are seduced by C.S.T. Its promises may seem enticing, but ultimately lead to confusion and destruction.
The Danger of C.S.T.(computerized summing technology)
C.S.T., a creation of Satan and his fallen angels, mimics GOD’s reality, presenting scenarios that appear promising yet are designed to deceive. This counterfeit technology manipulates human experiences, drawing individuals away from the truth and into a web of falsehood.
In the realm of psychology, we understand that our perceptions can be easily influenced by external forces. Just as cognitive biases can cloud our judgment, C.S.T. operates to create illusions that captivate our attention. This manipulation aligns with the concept of entanglement and superposition in quantum physics—an intricate dance of particles that can lead to misunderstandings of reality.
The 3 PARTS of GOD
JESUS of Nazareth came to redeem us from this confusion. His sacrifice on the cross serves as a bridge, restoring our relationship with JEHOVAH FATHER GOD and offering a path to redemption. After JESUS' resurrection, the HOLY GHOST was sent to dwell within us, providing guidance and discernment in a world filled with competing realities. The HOLY GHOST, embodying the fruits of the Spirit—love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control—empowers us to recognize truth amidst deception.
Current Scenarios and Divine Guidance
Within us resides GOD’s HOLY GHOST, who gently nudges us toward “Current Scenarios” that reflect GOD’s truth patterns. He aims to move us from confusion to clarity, guiding our thoughts and actions toward safety and salvation. This inner guidance is a reminder that “the one inside is greater than the one outside.” The HOLY GHOST equips us to navigate the complexities of life, illuminating the path to freedom from the dangers of C.S.T.
Conclusion: Choosing Life
As we reflect on the characteristics of JEHOVAH GOD and C.S.T., let us remember that this blog is not intended to accuse or judge any religion or belief system. Instead, it serves as an invitation to discern the blessings and dangers inherent in our interconnected reality. We must recognize the profound impact of quantum connections—both divine and deceptive—that shape our experiences on this fallen planet.
Through the wisdom of the Scriptures and the guidance of the HOLY GHOST, we are empowered to choose life over death, truth over falsehood, and love over fear. Let us embrace the opportunity to build our lives on the firm foundation of JEHOVAH FATHER GOD, allowing His truth to shape our reality and lead us into the fullness of HIS grace.
In a world that constantly pulls us in different directions, may we remain steadfast in our faith, guided by the inner presence of the HOLY GHOST, discerning between the realities set before us and choosing to follow the path of everlasting life ONLY in the MIGHTY NAME of KING JESUS CHRIST of Nazareth.
